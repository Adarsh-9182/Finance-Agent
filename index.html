FAQ

Fair questions.

Is it safe to connect my bank accounts?

Connections run through the RBI-regulated Account Aggregator framework (the same rails used by major lenders), with read-only access. Paisa never sees your banking passwords, can never move money, and you can revoke consent or delete your data at any time.

Does the AI give investment advice?

No. Paisa explains, computes and reminds — your 80C headroom, your SIP returns, your spend patterns. It's educational and organisational, not a registered investment adviser, and it won't tell you which stock to buy.

What can I ask it, really?

Anything answerable from your data: "How much on Zomato this year?", "Which policy renews next?", "Can I afford this EMI?", "What changed versus last month?" If it's not in your data, it says so instead of guessing.

Why is there a free plan?

Because trust is earned. Track manually, ask 30 questions a month, and upgrade only when auto-sync and the tax tracker feel worth ₹199 — about one plate of momos a week.
